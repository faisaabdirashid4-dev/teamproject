About East Africa University - Qardho

East Africa University's Qardho campus delivers academic programs focused on leadership, technology, and professional development. We emphasize applied learning, community engagement, and research relevant to the Horn of Africa.

Faculties & Programs

Faculty of Business & Economics

Programs: BBA, Accounting, Economics, Banking & Finance. Practical internships and entrepreneurship support.

Faculty of Computer Science

Programs: BSc Computer Science, IT, Software Engineering. Labs, coding clubs, and short courses.

Faculty of Social Sciences

Programs: Law, Political Science, Development Studies. Strong community outreach and research projects.
